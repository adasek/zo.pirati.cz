You can look forward to meeting many inspiring guests both from the Czech Republic and abroad.

The coronavirus crisis has demonstrated the importance of digitalization in public services, schools, and hospitals, allowing us to communicate with these institutions online. Digital technologies were also critical for us to maintain contacts with our families, friends, and colleagues during the pandemic. Last but not least, these technologies have significantly contributed to curbing the spread of the disease, as they were used for contact tracing or diagnosing patients.

However, our increased dependence on digital technologies has also exacerbated existing fears about the privacy and security of Internet users. The pandemic has exposed well known threats, such as disinformation, cybercrime, the monopolization of the Internet, and excessive governmental control. The goal of the Going Digital conference organized by the Pirate Party is to raise public awareness regarding the opportunities and threats represented by digital technologies in corona times. 

Besides the pandemic itself, the need to find smart solutions has become even more urgent due to the climate crisis. The wave of solidarity our societies experienced at the start of the pandemic has demonstrated that broad public engagement is essential for successfully overcoming both crises. Therefore, governments and legislators should strive to engage all citizens and seek the broadest possible consensus on our current challenges.

Digital democracy lies at the very core of our party’s functioning. We believe that including the public in decision-making through the Internet can strengthen both civil society and political participation of the general public. This is because only a well-educated and informed society is capable of making qualified decisions.

The struggle for a free Internet without surveillance and disinformation is the cornerstone of our political action. The future belongs to digitalization, the future belongs to the Pirates. We are looking forward to meeting you in December.
